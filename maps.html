Conflict Maps

Visualizing the geographic distribution of human suffering.

1. Intensity by Region (Choropleth)

This map illustrates the intensity of conflict across broader regions. Darker shades of blue indicate a higher accumulation of reported incidents.

2. Specific Events (Proportional Symbols)

Each circle represents a specific conflict event. The size of the circle is proportional to the human toll (civilian fatalities), highlighting the magnitude of individual tragedies.

3. Density of Crisis (Heatmap)

An organic representation of conflict concentration. Soft gradients reveal where crises overlap and intensify.
